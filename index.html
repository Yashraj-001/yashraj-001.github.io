01 — Origin Story

The Journey
That Built This

September 2022
Day One — Poornima University, Jaipur

I began my BTech in Cybersecurity at Poornima University. In the early months, the path was familiar — coursework, textbooks, the standard academic progression. But a quiet, persistent question began to surface: if every student is following the same track, what constitutes a genuine edge? That question refused to settle, and it eventually changed the direction of everything that followed.

2023
A Word I Did Not Yet Know — Hackathon

During 2023, I encountered a term I had not come across before: hackathon. The concept was vague to me at first — I understood it involved rapid building and competitive development, but little beyond that. Simultaneously, I was drawn to blockchain technology — the architecture of trustless systems felt like the logical evolution of security thinking. Both ideas stayed with me as 2023 came to a close.

March 2024
First Hackathon — Udaipur

In March 2024, I attended my first hackathon in Udaipur. I arrived with almost no preparation. What I found was something I had not anticipated — the pace, the focus, the collective intensity. I left with one clear conviction: I needed to do this again, more seriously.

First hackathon complete
July 2024
Blockchain Hackathon — Jaipur

A two-day blockchain-focused hackathon held in my home city. I was still learning, still finding my footing. But the experience taught me something valuable: the gap between where I was and where I wanted to be was not a question of innate ability — it was a question of application.

4–9 August 2024
Hacker House Goa — Selected and Invited

Selected through Devfolio by building on the DIAM blockchain. Five days residential. Late-night debugging sessions, genuine collaboration, networks formed under shared exhaustion. The most formative competitive experience I had encountered.

Most formative hackathon experience — Hacker House Goa

A Solidity lending platform with security logic embedded at the contract level. Top 10 from 150+ teams, $400 bounty won. The sponsor company indicated they intended to incorporate the code into their platform.

$350 Bounty · Cross-Chain Lending with Agoric Orchestration

3-Day Event · 0xDay · Pondicherry · Security Community

Personal Production Project · Independent Build · 2025

Project Horizon

A unified Zero Trust security architecture built entirely on open-source tooling. Five integrated pillars: ZTNA and SASE via Pomerium and Keycloak; automated PII discovery via Apache NiFi and Microsoft Presidio (DSPM); containerised workload security on K3s with Trivy and Falco (CNAPP); a unified XDR pipeline routing Suricata, Wazuh, and Cloud Custodian logs into the ELK Stack; and an insider risk analytics module.

Fully mapped to NIST 800-53 and MITRE ATT&CK, validated through live adversary simulations.
