// 01 — Origin Story

The Journey
That Built This

September 2022
Day One — Poornima University, Jaipur

Started BTech in Cybersecurity. Went the route everyone goes — classes, textbooks, the standard path. But if everyone's running the same race, what's actually the edge? That question wouldn't stay quiet.

2023
A Word I Didn't Know: Hackathon

Came across something called a hackathon. Didn't know exactly what it meant — just knew it was fast, intense, and had something to do with building under pressure. Blockchain fascinated me at the same time. The idea of a trustless system felt like the logical future of security.

March 2024
First Hackathon — Udaipur

Walked in knowing nothing. No idea about the format, the rules, what to build. But something lit up in that room — the energy, the pace, the people all-in on something. Left knowing this was exactly what I needed more of.

First hackathon complete
July 2024
Blockchain Hackathon — Jaipur

Two-day blockchain event in my own city. Still learning, still figuring things out — but it made me realize the limit wasn't ability. It was effort. The gap was closeable.

4–9 August 2024
Hacker House Goa — Selected & Invited

Found it on Devfolio. To get in, you had to build a real project on a sponsor blockchain — built on DIAM, got shortlisted and invited. Five days of residential hacking, late-night coding, real team dynamics, real networking. Didn't place — but learned more than any course could give.

Best hackathon experience of my life
September 2024
Gurgaon Hackathon — First Bounty Won · $400

Applied using the Goa project. Got shortlisted. Built a blockchain lending platform in Solidity — smart contracts with security logic embedded. Landed Top 10 out of 150+ teams, won $400 for best use of the sponsor blockchain. But more than the money — the company said they'd use the code in their own platform. That feeling? Indescribable.

$400 bounty · Top 10 / 150+ teams · Code adopted by sponsor
December 2024
Bangalore Hackathon — Second Bounty Won · $350

36-hour event on Agoric blockchain. Built a cross-chain lending platform — deposit any asset as collateral, borrow in a different currency with automatic conversion via orchestration, minimal gas fees. Won $350. Two bounties in a row. The pattern was becoming undeniable.

$350 bounty · Cross-chain lending platform
January 2025
IIT Madras Finalist — Shaastra Coding Contest

Applied to Shaastra (Coding Shastra), whose finals were held at IIT Madras — one of India's most prestigious institutions. Used logical reasoning over raw speed. Made the finals. Competed at IIT Madras. Checked off a dream.

IIT Madras Finalist · Shaastra Programming Contest
Feb 2025 – Nov 2025
Gardiyan — Full Cybersecurity Immersion

Joined Gardiyan after a competitive selection. One month of observation, then full internship. SOC operations, playbooks, daily log analysis on Splunk & ELK, detection rules in KQL, Azure Sentinel, AWS GuardDuty, real client tickets with 45-minute SLAs, night shifts, sleepless builds — and two production-grade security platforms as client deliverables. Received a full-time offer based on performance.

Full-time offer received · Based on performance
April 2026
Graduated — BTech Cybersecurity · CGPA 7.31

Completed all eight semesters. Passed from Poornima University in April 2026. Degree printing in December 2026. Four years of studying security, understanding systems, winning hackathons, and building production platforms alongside coursework.

CGPA 7.31 · Degree December 2026

Production MVP · Client Deliverable · 2025

Project Horizon

A unified Zero Trust security architecture built entirely on open-source tooling. Five integrated pillars — ZTNA & SASE via Pomerium and Keycloak for identity-aware access, automated PII discovery and DLP via Apache NiFi and Microsoft Presidio (DSPM), containerised workload security on K3s with Trivy and Falco (CNAPP), a unified XDR pipeline routing Suricata, Wazuh, and Cloud Custodian logs into ELK Stack, and an insider risk module. Demonstrates enterprise-grade Zero Trust at prototype scale.
